ABOUT US

Welcome to PAWS, the premier destination for individuals and families looking to bring a furry friend into their lives through dog adoption. Our mission is to connect loving homes with lovable dogs, fostering a lifetime of companionship and joy. Our dedicated team is committed to not only finding homes for dogs in need but also ensuring that each adoption is a successful and fulfilling match for both the adopter and the dog.When you adopt from PAWS, you're not just welcoming a new pet into your home; you're also making a difference in the lives of shelter dogs.
